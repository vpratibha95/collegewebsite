Junior Commerce-Academics

The Junior College offers courses in Science and Commerce prescribed by the Maharashtra state Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education at the 10+2 level.The courses lead to the Higher secondary Certificate Examination in the respective streams.

Duration

2 years

Awarding Authority :

Maharashtra State Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education, Pune.

COMMERCE – F.Y.J.C & S.Y.J.C (Std XI & XII)

  • English
  • Hindi or Marathi or French
  • Economics
  • Organization of Commerce
  • Book Keeping & Accountancy
  • Mathematics & Statistics / Secretarial Practice
  • I.T.** (Intake 240 seats)

    • Scheme of Evaluation

    Examinations are conducted as per the rules laid down by the State HSC Board. The Preliminary examination for S.Y.J.C Students is compulsary. The F.Y.J.C students will appear for four examination viz 2 Unit Tests

    (a) One terminal test
    (b) at the end of the first term and one final examination
    (c) at the end of the year.

    Admission & Eligibility – Junior College (Commerce)

  • Admission to First year in Junior college (FYJC – Std XI) is offered following the declaration of result of S.S.C. examination by the Maharashtra State Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education. The admissions are given strictly as per the directions issued by the Department of Education, Govt. of Maharashtra from time to time.
  • The candidates who have passed the S.S.C. examination of Maharashtra Board of Secondary & Higher Secondary Education or an examination regarded as equivalent by the State Board are eligible for admission to F.Y.J.C. (Std XI) class.
  • The students who have passed the equivalent examination as mentioned above will have to produce a provisional eligibility certificate issued by the Divisional Secretary, Maharashtra State Board of Secondary Education, Sector no. 16, Vashi, Navi Mumbai- 400073.
  • The eligible candidates have to apply in the prescribed form made available after the declaration of S.S.C. result.
  • The schedule of admission as prescribed by the Deputy Director (Education), Dept. of education, Govt. of Maharashtra will be strictly followed.
  • Students passing Std. X from CBSE and ICSE Boards will be considered at par with those who have passed the S.S.C. examination of the State Board.

    • Documents to be submitted along with admission form

  • Statement of Marks 2 attested copies (Original to be shown at the time of admission).
  • School Leaving Certificate 1 attested copy (Original to be shown at the time of admission).
  • * Caste Certificate – 1 attested copy (Original to be shown at the time of admission).
